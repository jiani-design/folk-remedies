    Mexico: Chamomile Tea/Manzanilla

    Chamomile tea (Manzanilla) is a traditional herbal remedy in Mexican culture for upset stomach symptoms as it acts as an anti-inflammatory for our digestive system.

    Ingredients:

    • Fresh/dried chamomile
    • Water
    • Honey

    Steps:

    1. Prepare 3 cups of water to boil.
    2. Add a handful of chamomile herbal to the boiling water.
    3. Cover the water and let it steep for 5 minutes.
    4. Strain the chamomile flowers, then add some honey to sweeten it.

    Ethiopia: Telba Drink

    Telba drink is a traditional Ethiopian drink made with ground flaxseed. The flaxseed is believed to soothe the digestive system while relieving constipation.

    Ingredients:

    • Flaxseed powder
    • Water
    • Honey
    • Boiling water

    Steps:

    1. Grind the flax seeds and mix them with a little bit of water until they form a thick paste.
    2. Add a spoonful of honey to the paste.
    3. Stir and mix.
    4. Pour a cup of boiling water into the paste and wait for the drink to cool down.
